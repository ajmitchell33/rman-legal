Delete Your RMAN Account

You can delete your RMAN account and all associated data at any time, directly from within the app or by contacting us. Deletion is permanent and cannot be undone.

Warning: Deleting your account permanently removes all your data. This action cannot be reversed.

How to Delete Your Account In-App

  1. Open the RMAN app and sign in.
  2. Tap your profile icon in the top right corner of the Partners screen.
  3. Scroll to the bottom and tap Delete Account.
  4. Confirm the deletion when prompted.

What Gets Deleted

When you delete your account, the following data is permanently removed:

  • Your account credentials and profile information
  • All partner profiles and relationship notes
  • All AI-generated suggestions and saved ideas
  • All memory vault facts Arman has learned
  • All shared links and saved content
  • All chat history with Arman
  • Your profile photo

What Is Not Immediately Deleted

Anonymized analytics and crash reports held by Firebase Analytics and Firebase Crashlytics may be retained for up to 60 days per Google's standard data retention policies. These records cannot be linked back to you after your account is deleted.

Active Subscriptions

Deleting your account does not automatically cancel an active RMAN Pro subscription. To avoid future charges, please cancel your subscription through the Google Play Store before deleting your account:

  1. Open the Google Play Store app.
  2. Tap your profile icon → Payments & subscriptions → Subscriptions.
  3. Select RMAN and tap Cancel subscription.

Request Deletion by Email

If you are unable to access the app, you can request account deletion by emailing us at aj@tcbbcreativeagency.com with the subject line "Account Deletion Request" and the email address associated with your account. We will process your request within 30 days.
