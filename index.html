Privacy Policy

RMAN — AI Relationship Manager

Last Updated: April 27, 2026

1. Introduction

RMAN ("we," "our," or "us") is an AI-powered relationship management application ("App") that helps you maintain and deepen the relationships that matter most to you. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding that information.

By using RMAN, you agree to the practices described in this policy. If you do not agree, please discontinue use of the App.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide Directly

  • Account information: Your name, email address, and profile photo when you register or sign in (including via Google Sign-In or Sign in with Apple on iOS).
  • Partner profiles: Names, relationship types, ages, interests, important dates, notes, and profile photos you add for people in your life.
  • AI conversation content: Messages, questions, and context you provide when interacting with Arman, our AI assistant. This content is sent to Google's Gemini API to generate suggestions and responses.
  • Todos and suggestions: Activity ideas, gift suggestions, date ideas, recipes, and other content you save or mark complete within the App.
  • Shared links: URLs you share into RMAN from other apps (via the iOS Share Extension or Android share sheet). We extract and store metadata (title, description, preview image) for display within the App.
  • Shared partner profiles: When you use the "Share a Person" feature, a sanitized snapshot of that partner's profile (name, relationship type, interests, location, and important dates — but not private notes, AI suggestions, or Memory Vault facts) is temporarily stored in our database and made accessible via a unique invite code. This snapshot is readable by anyone who has the code, including unauthenticated users viewing the web preview page. Shared profiles expire automatically after 30 days.

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Usage data: How you interact with the App, including features used, screens viewed, and actions taken, collected via Firebase Analytics.
  • Device information: Device type, operating system version, app version, and general location (country/region) collected via Firebase Analytics.
  • Crash and performance data: Error logs and performance metrics collected via Firebase Crashlytics to help us identify and fix bugs.

2.3 Information from Third-Party Services

  • Google Sign-In: If you sign in with Google, we receive your Google account name, email address, and profile photo. We do not receive access to other Google account data.
  • Sign in with Apple (iOS only): If you sign in with Apple, we receive your Apple-provided name and email address (Apple may relay a private email address at your option). We do not receive any other Apple account data.
  • Google Calendar (if you grant permission): Upcoming calendar events to help surface relevant reminders. This data is read-only and is not stored on our servers.
  • YouTube: The Partner Feed feature queries YouTube's Data API server-side using your partner's interests as search terms. We receive video titles, thumbnails, and channel names. Your identity is not shared with YouTube.
  • Giphy: The Partner Feed may include GIFs sourced from the Giphy API, searched using your partner's interests. Your identity is not shared with Giphy.
  • ZenQuotes: Inspirational quotes displayed in the Partner Feed are fetched from the ZenQuotes public API. No personal data is shared with ZenQuotes.
  • Facebook (event metadata only): If you share a Facebook event link into RMAN, our server-side function may query the Facebook Graph API to retrieve the event name, description, and cover image for display. Your Facebook credentials are not used for this — only our app's server-side access token is involved.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide the App: Create and manage your account, store your partner profiles, and deliver AI-generated suggestions personalized to the people you care about.
  • Power the AI (Arman): Your partner profiles, interests, notes, and conversation messages are sent to Google's Gemini API to generate contextually relevant suggestions. Google processes these prompts subject to their own terms and privacy policy.
  • Sync your data: Your profiles, suggestions, and saved content are stored in Firebase Firestore and Firebase Storage so your data is available across devices and is not lost if you reinstall the App.
  • Improve the App: Aggregated, anonymized analytics data (via Firebase Analytics) helps us understand how the App is used and where to improve.
  • Send notifications: With your permission, we may send push notifications for daily suggestion reminders and important date reminders.
  • Authenticate your identity: Firebase Auth and Facebook Login are used to securely identify you and protect your account.

We do not use your data to serve third-party advertising. We do not sell your personal information.

5. Data Storage and Retention

Where Your Data Is Stored

Your data is stored on Google Firebase infrastructure (Firestore and Firebase Storage), primarily in US-based data centers. By using the App, you consent to this transfer and storage.

How Long We Retain Your Data

  • Active accounts: Your data is retained for as long as your account is active.
  • Deleted accounts: When you delete your account within the App, we initiate deletion of your data from Firestore and Firebase Storage within 30 days. Some aggregate, anonymized analytics data may be retained for longer periods as it cannot be associated with you.
  • Inactive accounts: Accounts with no activity for 24 months may be flagged for deletion. We will attempt to notify you before deletion.
  • Local device data: The App stores some data locally on your device (in a local database and app storage). This data is removed when you sign out or uninstall the App.

6. AI Data and the Gemini API

When you use Arman's AI features, the following data is sent to Google's Gemini API:

  • Your partner's name, relationship type, age, interests, and notes
  • Important dates associated with that partner
  • Recent memory facts Arman has learned
  • Your message or request

Google's use of this data is governed by the Google Cloud Terms of Service and Google's AI/ML Privacy Notice. By default, Google does not use API data submitted through the Gemini API to train their models.

We do not store the raw text of AI prompts or responses beyond what is needed to display them within the App session.

7. Profile Photos

Profile photos you upload for yourself or your partners are stored in Firebase Storage. These photos are:

  • Private to your account and not publicly accessible
  • Retained for as long as your account is active
  • Deleted when you remove the associated partner profile or delete your account

If we become aware that we have collected data from a child under 13, we will delete it promptly. If you believe we have inadvertently collected data from a child, please contact us at the email address below.

  • Deletion: You may delete your account and associated data at any time through the App settings. You may also contact us to request deletion.
  • Correction: You may update or correct your information at any time within the App.
  • Data portability: You may request your data in a portable format.

For Users in the European Economic Area (EEA) and UK — GDPR Rights

Under the General Data Protection Regulation, you additionally have the right to:

  • Restrict processing: Request that we limit how we use your data.
  • Object to processing: Object to certain types of processing, including profiling.
  • Withdraw consent: Where processing is based on consent, you may withdraw it at any time.
  • Lodge a complaint: You have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority.

Our legal basis for processing your data is contract performance (to provide the App), legitimate interests (improving the App, preventing fraud), and consent (notifications, optional features).

For California Residents — CCPA Rights

Under the California Consumer Privacy Act, you have the right to:

  • Know what personal information we collect, use, disclose, and sell (we do not sell your data).
  • Delete your personal information (subject to certain exceptions).
  • Opt out of the sale of personal information (we do not sell your data).
  • Non-discrimination: We will not discriminate against you for exercising your CCPA rights.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at the email address below. We will respond within 30 days (GDPR) or 45 days (CCPA) of your request.
